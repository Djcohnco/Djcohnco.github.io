About Me

Hi I'm Dan! I am a tech lover based in New Jersey. I currently work as an information security analyst at American Express where I have worked as a risk advisor to senior technology leaders, an information security subject matter expert for marketing and brand initiatives, and more recently have worked on developing a risk rating framework for large scale technology transformation projects, based on the CRI Profile. Prior to my tenure at American Express, I spent time as a security analyst and systems administrator at Trinity Solar from 2021-2022 and time as an IT Technician at Data Safe LLC from 2019-2021 after graduating from Penn State in 2019 with a degree in Security and Risk Analysis with a focus in Information and Cyber Security (We Are!)
I am a lover of all things tech, I love building (and breaking) things to expand my knowledge and become a more well rounded technologist. I love learning more about tech and have never backed down from a challenge.
