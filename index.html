About Me

Hi, I'm Dan! I am passionate about all things tech, and I enjoy building (and breaking) things to expand my knowledge and become a more well-rounded technologist. I love learning about technology and never back down from a challenge.
I'm an Information Security Analyst based in New Jersey working for American Express. Recently, I've been developing a risk rating framework for large-scale technology transformation projects based on the CRI Profile. I have served as a risk advisor to senior technology leaders and as an information security subject matter expert for marketing and brand initiatives. Before my tenure at American Express, I worked as a Security Analyst and Systems Administrator at Trinity Solar from 2021 to 2022, and as an IT Technician at Data Safe LLC from 2019 to 2021. I graduated from Penn State in 2019 with a degree in Security and Risk Analysis, focusing on Information and Cyber Security (We Are!).
