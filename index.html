About Me

Hi, I'm Dan—an information security professional with a passion for technology, automation, and solving complex problems. I'm currently a Senior Information Security Analyst in Technology Control Management at American Express, where I focus on identifying and managing technology risks within the company. While my role is centered around risk advisory and governance, I have a strong interest in the technical side of security—understanding how systems work, where they break, and how they can be made more resilient.
I'm also pursuing my Master of Science in Cybersecurity at Georgia Tech, where I'm expanding my expertise in risk management, system security, and emerging threats. Outside of work and school, I enjoy building and breaking things—whether it's automating processes, experimenting with new technologies, or tinkering in my homelab to test new security concepts.
I'm always looking for ways to bridge the gap between security, business, and technology. If you're working on something interesting in automation, security, or breaking things for fun (ethically, of course), let's connect.
